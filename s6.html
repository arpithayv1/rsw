LEMON PICKLE
Ingredients:

  • 8-10 medium-sized lemons
  • 1/4 cup mustard oil
  • 2 tbsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tbsp fenugreek seeds
  • 2 tsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
Instructions:
  1. Cut lemons into pieces, remove seeds.
  2. Dry roast and grind mustard seeds and fenugreek seeds.
  3. Mix lemons with spice powder, chili powder, turmeric, asafoetida, and salt.
  4. Pour cooled mustard oil over lemons.
  5. Mix well, store in airtight jar, let marinate for days.
