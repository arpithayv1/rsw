RICE PAYASAM
Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • A pinch of cardamom powder
  • A handful of raisins and chopped nuts (e.g., cashews, almonds)
  • Ghee (clarified butter) for frying the nuts (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Cook rice until soft and mashable.
  2. In a separate pot, boil milk and add cooked rice. Simmer until it thickens.
  3. Add sugar and cardamom powder. Stir well.
  4. In a separate pan, fry the raisins and chopped nuts in ghee until they puff up and turn golden.
  5. Add the fried raisins and nuts to the payasam.
  6. Serve hot or cold.
