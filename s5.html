MANGO PICKLE
Ingredients:

  • 2 medium-sized raw mangoes, cut into pieces
  • 1 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tbsp mustard seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tbsp fenugreek seeds
  • 1/4 cup mustard oil
Instructions:
  1. Cut mangoes into pieces.
  2. Dry roast and grind mustard seeds and fenugreek seeds.
  3. Mix mangoes with spice powder, chili powder, turmeric, asafoetida, and salt.
  4. Pour cooled mustard oil over mangoes.
  5. Mix well, store in airtight jar, let marinate for days.
