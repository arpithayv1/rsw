Sabudana Payasam
Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup sabudana (tapioca pearls)
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • A pinch of cardamom powder
  • A handful of chopped nuts (e.g., cashews, almonds)
  • Ghee (clarified butter) for frying the nuts (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Wash and soak sabudana in water for a few hours until they become soft.
  2. Boil milk and add soaked sabudana. Simmer until sabudana is cooked and the mixture thickens.
  3. Add sugar and cardamom powder. Stir well.
  4. In a separate pan, fry the chopped nuts in ghee until golden brown.
  5. Add fried nuts to the payasam.
  6. Serve hot or cold.
