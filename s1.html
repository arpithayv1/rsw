CARROT PICKLE
Ingredients:

  • 500g carrots, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp mustard seeds (coarsely ground)
  • 1 tbsp fenugreek seeds (coarsely ground)
  • 1 tbsp fennel seeds
  • 1/2 cup mustard oil
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • 2-3 dried red chilies
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
Instructions:
  1. Roast mustard, fenugreek, and fennel seeds; then grind coarsely.
  2. Heat mustard oil until smoking, then cool slightly.
  3. Mix carrots, turmeric, red chili, salt, ground spices, asafoetida, dried chilies, and vinegar.
  4. Pour cooled mustard oil over the mixture, ensuring it's fully covered.
  5. Store in a glass jar, let sit for 2-3 days at room temperature, then refrigerate.
