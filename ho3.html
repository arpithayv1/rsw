Carrot Halwa
Ingredients:

  • Ghee
  • Nuts
  • Cardamom Powder
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Carrots
  • Saffron (optional)
  • Raisins (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Saut√© grated carrots in ghee until soft.
  2. Add milk, cook until thickened.
  3. Stir in sugar, nuts, cardamom, and saffron (optional).
  4. Cook until it leaves the sides of the pan.
  5. Garnish with fried raisins (optional).
  6. Serve warm. Enjoy carrot halwa!
