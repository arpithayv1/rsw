Kayi Holige
Ingredients:

  • For the Filling:
    • 2 cups grated coconut
    • 1 cup jaggery (or to taste)
    • A pinch of cardamom powder
  • For the Dough:
    • 1 cup maida (all-purpose flour)
    • A pinch of salt
    • Water as needed
    • Ghee for frying
Instructions:
  1. Mix grated coconut, jaggery, and cardamom powder to make the filling.
  2. For the dough, mix maida, salt, and enough water to make a soft dough.
  3. Divide dough and filling into equal portions.
  4. Roll out dough portions, place filling, and seal to form flatbreads.
  5. Cook with ghee until golden brown.
