Rava Kesribath
Ingredients:

  • Semolina (Sooji/Rava)
  • Sugar
  • Ghee
  • Nuts
  • Cardamom
  • Saffron (optional)
  • Food Color (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Roast semolina in ghee, add sugar, boiling water, cardamom, saffron (if used), and food color (if used)
  2. Cook until thickened, add nuts, and serve warm.
