Badam Halwa
Ingredients:

  • Ghee
  • Almonds
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Saffron (optional)
  • Nuts
  • Cardamom
Instructions:
  1. Grind almonds with milk to a paste.
  2. Cook almond paste in ghee until thick.
  3. Add sugar, saffron, and cook until halwa forms.
  4. Mix in cardamom.
  5. Garnish with nuts.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy Badam Halwa!
