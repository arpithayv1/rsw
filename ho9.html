PineappleHalwa
Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of fresh pineapple puree
  • 1 cup of sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons of ghee (clarified butter)
  • A handful of chopped nuts (like cashews and almonds)
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cardamom powder
  • A pinch of saffron strands (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Heat ghee in a pan and add pineapple puree.
  2. Cook the pineapple puree until it thickens and reduces in volume.
  3. Add sugar and continue cooking until the halwa thickens and leaves the sides of the pan.
  4. Stir in cardamom powder and saffron (if using).
  5. Add chopped nuts and mix well.
  6. Remove from heat and serve warm. Pineapple halwa is ready to enjoy!
