Onion Stir-Fry
Ingredients:

  • Onions, thinly sliced
  • Garlic cloves, minced
  • Oil for cooking
  • Salt to taste
  • Green chilies, chopped (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Heat oil in a pan, add sliced onions.
  2. Saut√© until onions turn translucent.
  3. Add minced garlic and green chilies, if using.
  4. Season with salt and continue cooking until onions caramelize.
