GARLIC PICKLE
Ingredients:

  • 200g peeled garlic cloves
  • 1/4 cup mustard oil
  • 2 tbsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tbsp fenugreek seeds
  • 2 tsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
  • Juice of 2 lemons
Instructions:
  1. Dry roast mustard seeds and fenugreek seeds, then grind into a coarse powder.
  2. Combine garlic cloves with spice powder, red chili powder, turmeric, asafoetida, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Pour cooled mustard oil over the mixture, add lemon juice.
  4. Mix well, transfer to a jar, and let it sit for days to develop flavors.
