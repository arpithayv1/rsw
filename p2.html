SPROUTS SALAD
Ingredients:

  • Mixed sprouts (e.g., moong, chickpea, lentils)
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Diced onions
  • Diced cucumber
  • Lemon juice to taste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
Instructions:
  1. Combine sprouted legumes with diced vegetables.
  2. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
