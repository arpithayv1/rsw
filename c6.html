Rajma Masala
Ingredients:

  • 1 cup kidney beans (rajma), soaked and cooked
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
Instructions:
  1. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onion and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, chopped tomato, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add cooked kidney beans and garam masala. Simmer for a while.
  5. Garnish with chopped cilantro if desired.
  6. Serve hot with rice or chapati.
