KNIFE SKILLS

Types of Knives: Chef's knives, paring knives, and others have specific uses.
Holding the Knife: Grip it firmly, with index finger and thumb near the blade's base.
Cuts : Learn to julienne, dice, mince, chop, slice, and peel.
Claw Technique: Protect fingers by curling them while using knuckles to guide the knife
Practice Skill improves with practice, starting with basic cuts.

PREPARATION

Chilling: Refrigerate perishables promptly and maintain correct temperatures.
Labels and Expiration Dates: Pay attention to food labels and expiration dates.
Leftovers: Store leftovers properly and consume them within a reasonable time.
Hand Hygiene: Wash hands before handling food.
Food Allergies: Be mindful of food allergies and cross-contact.

FOOD SAFETY

Gather Ingredients: Collect all necessary ingredients before cooking.
Measure Accurately: Use measuring tools for precise quantities, especially in baking.
Wash and Clean: Rinse fruits and vegetables, scrub when needed.
Season and Taste: Add herbs, spices, and seasonings as desired. Taste as you go.
Presentation: Arrange dishes attractively and garnish for visual appeal.
