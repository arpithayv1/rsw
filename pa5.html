BADAM KHEER
Ingredients:

  • 1 cup almonds, soaked and peeled
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • A pinch of cardamom powder
  • A few saffron strands (soaked in warm milk)
  • A handful of chopped nuts (e.g., cashews, pistachios)
  • Ghee (clarified butter) for frying the nuts (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Grind soaked and peeled almonds into a smooth paste.
  2. Boil milk and add almond paste. Simmer until it thickens.
  3. Add sugar, cardamom powder, and saffron-infused milk. Stir well.
  4. In a separate pan, fry the chopped nuts in ghee until golden.
  5. Add fried nuts to the kheer.
  6. Serve hot or cold.
