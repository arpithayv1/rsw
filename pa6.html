OATS PAYASAM
Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • A pinch of cardamom powder
  • A handful of chopped nuts (e.g., cashews, almonds)
  • Ghee (clarified butter) for frying the nuts (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Roast oats in ghee until they become aromatic and slightly brown.
  2. Boil milk and add roasted oats. Simmer until it thickens.
  3. Add sugar and cardamom powder. Stir well.
  4. In a separate pan, fry the chopped nuts in ghee until golden.
  5. Add fried nuts to the payasam.
  6. Serve hot or cold.
