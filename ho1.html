Bele Holige
Ingredients:

  • For the Filling:
    • 1 cup chana dal (split Bengal gram)
    • 1 cup jaggery (or to taste)
    • 1 tsp cardamom powder
  • For the Dough:
    • 1 cup whole wheat flour
    • A pinch of salt
    • Water as needed
    • Ghee for frying
Instructions:
  1. Cook chana dal until soft. Drain any excess water.
  2. Mix cooked dal, jaggery, and cardamom powder. Cook until it forms a thick mixture.
  3. For the dough, mix wheat flour, salt, and enough water to make a soft, pliable dough.
  4. Divide dough and filling into equal portions.
  5. Roll out dough portions, place filling, and seal to form flatbreads.
  6. Cook with ghee until golden brown.
