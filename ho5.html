Pumpkin Halwa
Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of grated pumpkin
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons of ghee (clarified butter)
  • A handful of chopped nuts (like cashews and almonds)
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cardamom powder
  • A pinch of saffron strands (optional)
  • A pinch of nutmeg powder (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Heat ghee in a pan and add the grated pumpkin.
  2. Saut√© the pumpkin until it becomes soft and translucent.
  3. Add milk and simmer until it thickens.
  4. Stir in sugar, cardamom, saffron (if using), and nutmeg (if using). Cook until it thickens further.
  5. Add chopped nuts and mix well.
  6. Remove from heat and serve warm. Pumpkin halwa is ready to enjoy!
