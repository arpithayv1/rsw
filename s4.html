VEGETABLE PICKLE
Ingredients:

  • Mixed vegetables (e.g., carrots, cauliflower, green beans)
  • 1/2 cup mustard oil
  • 2 tbsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tbsp fenugreek seeds
  • 2 tsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
  • Juice of 2 lemons
Instructions:
  1. Chop vegetables into pieces.
  2. Dry roast mustard seeds and fenugreek seeds, then grind into a coarse powder.
  3. Mix vegetables with spice powder, red chili powder, turmeric, asafoetida, and salt.
  4. Pour cooled mustard oil over the mixture, add lemon juice.
  5. Stir well, transfer to a jar, and let it sit for days to develop flavors.
