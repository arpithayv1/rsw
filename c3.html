PALAK PANEER
Ingredients:

  • 2 cups chopped spinach (palak)
  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cream (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Blanch the chopped spinach and blend into a smooth puree.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onion and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste, chopped tomato, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
  5. Add the spinach puree and cook for a few minutes.
  6. Add paneer cubes and garam masala. Simmer for a while.
  7. Optionally, add cream for richness.
  8. Serve hot.
