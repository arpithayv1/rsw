AMLA PICKLE
Ingredients:

  • 250g Amla (Indian gooseberries), chopped and deseeded
  • 2 tbsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tbsp fenugreek seeds
  • 1/2 cup mustard oil
  • 2 tsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
  • Juice of 2 lemons
Instructions:
  1. Dry roast mustard seeds and fenugreek seeds, then grind into a coarse powder.
  2. Mix chopped amla with the spice powder, red chili powder, turmeric, asafoetida, and salt.
  3. Pour cooled mustard oil over the mixture and add lemon juice.
  4. Stir well, transfer to a jar, and let it sit for a few days to develop flavors.
