BENDEKAYI PALYA
Ingredients:

  • 4-5 small brinjals (eggplants)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped cilantro for garnish (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Roast brinjals over an open flame or in the oven until the skin is charred and they become soft.
  2. Peel the charred skin, chop the roasted brinjals, and set aside.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds and mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  4. Add chopped brinjals, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Cook for a few minutes, garnish with cilantro, and serve.
