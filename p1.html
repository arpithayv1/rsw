Moong Dal Carrot Kosambari
Ingredients:

  • 1 cup moong dal (split green gram)
  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • Grated coconut
  • Lemon juice to taste
  • Salt to taste
Instructions:
  1. Soak moong dal for a few hours, then drain.
  2. Mix moong dal, grated carrots, green chilies, and grated coconut.
  3. Add lemon juice and salt to taste.
  4. Toss well and serve as a salad.
